§ 02
The Journey

Four phases, one person — watching her own return take shape.

Brooks Band observes nine AVVOF™ categories spanning physiological, behavioral, and environmental signals — movement, sleep, routine anchors, ambient sound, and more — quietly, privately, at her side. Margaret and the people she chose to share with see what's really happening, honestly.

01
Weeks 1–3 · The Quiet

Settle & observe

Home feels unfamiliar in a new way. Sleep is broken. Movement is careful. The ambient sound reading is strikingly low — a house that used to hum with music and company has gone still. The Actual Self avatar lags well behind the Ideal. Margaret sees her starting point honestly, without shame.

02
Weeks 4–6 · The Turn

Build rhythm

Sleep consolidates. Daily steps climb from 180 to 620. Margaret starts making her own coffee again — a behavioral signal the band notices as a returning rhythm. The Authenticity Score begins to rise.

03
Weeks 7–9 · The Return

Reclaim routines

Margaret walks to the mailbox for the first time. The band logs the outdoor time; MirrorMatch marks the goal met. Sleep is strong. The small habits that used to feel like her — they're beginning to feel like her again.

04
Weeks 10–12 · The Self

Integrate & sustain

The Authenticity Score holds at 0.89 — her actual life now closely matches the life she said she wanted. She hosts Thanksgiving for fourteen people. The house is louder than it's been in two years, and Margaret laughs when she sees the number. Lynn notices, on the phone, that her mother's voice sounds different.

§ 03
Privacy by Design

What happens on your wrist stays on your wrist.

Brooks Band™ was designed from the first line of code around a single premise: the person wearing it deserves technology that serves them, not technology that surveils them. Every AVVOF™ observation is processed on the device itself. Nothing is transmitted, stored in the cloud, or sold — ever.

You choose what, if anything, to share. You choose with whom. You choose when that permission ends. If you decide one day to share a weekly summary with a daughter, a wellness coach, a trusted friend, or a licensed professional such as your occupational therapist, that's your decision — made by you, revocable by you, at any time.

When you do choose to share your wellness observations with a licensed professional — an OT, a wellness coach, a provider you trust — they receive only what you explicitly authorize. What that professional does with those observations is a matter of their own training, licensure, and scope of practice. Brooks Band™ does not interpret, recommend, flag, alert, diagnose, prescribe, or direct care. It simply makes it possible for you to show the professionals you trust what you're already seeing on your own wrist. The professional brings the professional expertise. You bring you.

Principle · 01

On-Device Only

All observations are processed locally on the wearable. No cloud storage of biometric signals. No background telemetry.

Principle · 02

Consent-First

Nothing is ever shared without the wearer's explicit, per-recipient permission. Consent can be withdrawn at any moment.

Principle · 03

No Sale, No Ads

Sensory Bridges, LLC does not sell, rent, license, or advertise against wearer data. That is a permanent commitment.

Principle · 04

You Share, They Interpret

When you choose to share observations with a licensed professional, the band does not interpret. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or direct care. The professional brings their own training and scope of practice to what you've chosen to share.

Technology that treats people like people, not projects.
